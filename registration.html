Registration

Registration

Pre-registration is open here.
The attendance fee is 200€, which includes coffee breaks and workshop material.
For those who want to attend the social dinner, the fee is 250€.



Due to the current COVID-19 epidemic in Italy, for the time being there is no payment option. At a later date will and contact those who have pre-registered and/or submitted an abstract for the final registration and provide all the details for the payment.

Fee waivers

The journal Entropy will offer two fee waivers for young scholars that want to attend the conference. To apply for a waiver, send an email to liph.unipd@gmail.com before May 15th, 2020 12:00 CET, with subject "SMEEB 2020 Entropy free waivers" and your CV attached. A commission of independent scholars will evaluate the applications.

Abstract and poster submission

Submit an abstract for a contributed talk or a poster here (EasyChair).


The deadline for abstract and poster submissions is May 24-th, 2020 at 12:00 CET.


Notification of acceptance will be sent by May 27-th, 2020.
